03 — History

The Visionaries Behind the Brand

Karl Benz (1844–1929)

Inventor of the first gasoline-powered automobile — the Benz Patent-Motorwagen (1885). His pioneering work laid the foundation for modern automotive engineering.

Gottlieb Daimler (1834–1900)

Pioneer who developed the high-speed petrol engine and co-founded Daimler Motoren Gesellschaft, revolutionizing land, sea, and air transportation.

Wilhelm Maybach (1846–1929)

Chief engineer at Daimler who designed the first Mercedes automobile of 1901, often called "the king of designers" for his exceptional engineering talent.

07 — Motorsport Legacy

The Silver Arrows

  • Over 125 years of motorsport involvement — one of the most decorated racing brands in history
  • Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team: 8 consecutive Constructors' Championships (2014–2021)
  • Lewis Hamilton won 6 of his 7 World Championships with Mercedes, becoming the most decorated F1 driver
  • AMG division (Affalterbach) produces hand-built high-performance road cars since 1967
  • The "Silver Arrows" name dates to 1934 when the car's paint was stripped to meet weight limits, revealing bare aluminum
